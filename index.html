About

I am a third-year undergraduate student in Harbin Institute of Technology(HIT). I spent an unforgettable year in Networked Robotics and Systems Lab (NRSL) , where I was fortunate to work Prof. Haoyao Chen. I spent an unforgettable summer in 2020 doing internship at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Leonidas J. Guibas.
