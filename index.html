About

I am currently a third-year undergraduate student in Harbin Institute of Technology(HIT), under supervision of Prof.Zhihua Yang. I spent an unforgettable year in AI&Robot Lab at Tsinghua University(Shenzhen) , where I discovered my research interests in robotic perception and SLAM.

My research interests include robotic perception and simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM). I am also very interested in the application of machine learning to robot perception, becasue I think using machine learning to make machines perceive, understand and interact with their surroundings is the nonly way to fully autonomous driving.
