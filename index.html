About

I am currently a fourth-year undergraduate student in Harbin Institute of Technology(HIT), under supervision of Prof.Zhihua Yang. I spent an unforgettable year in AI&Robot Lab at Tsinghua University(Shenzhen) , where I discovered my research interests in robotic perception and SLAM. Recently I am working with Prof.Jun MA, and doing some research about motion planning and decision.

My research interests include robotic perception, motion planning and decision. Recently I am working on using reinforcement learning to make self-driving vehicles play games with other vehicles in complex dynamic environments. I think using machine learning to make robots perceive, understand and interact with their surroundings is a good way to solve related problems.
