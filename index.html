About

I am currently a third-year undergraduate student in Harbin Institute of Technology(HIT), under supervision of Prof.Zhihua Yang. I spent an unforgettable year in AI&Robot Lab at Tsinghua University(Shenzhen) , where I discovered my research interests in robotic perception and SLAM.

My research interests include robotic perception and simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM). I am also very interested in the application of machine learning to robot perception, becasue I think using machine learning to make machines perceive, understand and interact with their surroundings is the nonly way to fully autonomous driving.


Project Experience

  • [October, 2019--June, 2020] Arduino delivery trolley based on multiple indoor positioning technologies Undergraduate Research on Campus In this project, we mainly discuss several popular indoor positioning technologies and implement them respectively, and finally discuss the most suitable indoor positioning technology for delivery vehicles in our experimental scenario technique. The solutions we tried are as follows: (1) Use the geomagnetic sensor to judge the current angle of the car, and then use the RFID technology to set the label to clarify the route of the car, and complete the function of car delivery; (2) Use Bluetooth/WLAN technology and three-point positioning method for indoor positioning; (3) Indoor terrain modeling and direction navigation based on laser slam.
